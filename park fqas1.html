General Tips for Your Visit to Wonderla Kochi

  • Take breaks - stop before you get too tired
  • Do not force others to take a ride
  • Don't run inside the park
  • Most of the pools are shallow; don't dive in the pools
  • Guests with medical conditions should avoid rides that might impact their health
  • Parents are advised to pay special attention to their children: children should be accompanied by adults at all times
  • Follow height restrictions for the rides - they are for your safety
  • Remain in the ride until it comes to a complete stop at the unloading point. If a ride stops temporarily due to mechanical failure, stay seated and wait for the ride to start up again, or for an operator to assist you
  • Our staff is always available for help
