Food & Dining at Wonderla Park, Kochi

Wonderla Amusement Park, Kochi offers a variety of tasty dishes and lip-smacking food at our 10+ eateries, along with fruit shakes to beat the heat. It can easily accommodate over 1,000 people, ensuring that you will never have to stand around and wait for a table. It also houses one of the best snack counters and cafes in Kochi. Head over to the park for a fun-filled day with family, friends and loved ones, to enjoy thrilling rides and mouth-watering dishes.

Buffet lunch is also available at all three parks and the resort, upon prior booking!
