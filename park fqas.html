Wear Your Comfort at Wonderla

For your safety and hygiene, according to internationally-accepted standards, the permitted clothing materials have to be made of 100% nylon or synthetic materials.

Why dress code?

Our amusement park has several rides to have fun on. Almost all of them involve motion or moving parts that require a special dress code to ensure your health and safety. For instance, loose garments like sarees, salwar, dupatta, churidar, shawls or scarves could get entangled and lead to potential harm to yourselves and others. In order to ensure that the water quality for all rides is maintained, we enforce a strict dress code.

What's allowed

The appropriate outfits for our water rides are T-shirts, shorts, bermudas, three-fourths, swimsuits. The permitted clothing materials are made of 100% nylon or synthetic material.

What's not allowed

Clothes that put you at risk! The following types of outfits can put your health and safety at risk and are prohibited for water rides. Sarees, salwar, dupatta, formal pants, shirts, burkhas, shawls, scarves, school uniforms, jeans, three-fourths, cargoes, loose garments and anything other than the aforementioned list of permitted outfits.

In case you are not wearing or carrying garments that fit the bill, we offer suitable clothes that can be purchased from the park stores at a nominal price.

*Beware of sub-standard materials and low-quality products while buying from outside/anonymous sources. Visitors are advised to buy the specified attire for water rides at reasonable rates from park outlets.*

Have a great day ahead with lots of fun!
